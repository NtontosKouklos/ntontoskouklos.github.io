Intro

Dr Theodoros Marinopoulos is a Lecturer in Mechanical Engineering at Edge Hill University with a research background in structural integrity, computational mechanics, and advanced additive manufacturing. His work combines finite element analysis, topology optimisation, and experimental characterisation to improve the mechanical performance of additively manufactured polymers, metals, and fibre-reinforced composites. His research interests include multi-material additive manufacturing, lightweight and thermally efficient structural design, and the development of integrated experimental–numerical frameworks for high-performance engineering applications, including personalised prosthetics and sustainable manufacturing solutions.
